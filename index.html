The Happiness Tree

  1. What trends are noticed when comparing happiness across regions and years?
  2. How do the features specifically vary the overall scores?

This visualisation displays the Happiness Report (2005–2023) as a living tree, much like the idea of a tree of life. Each branch is a world region, each flower represents a country, and each petal shows one of the features that varies a countries happiness. Please explore all the interactive features to see what matters the most to countries. Hover or click on flowers for more details, select branches to view whole regions, and finally use the year slider to watch how happiness develops with time. Every country has its own flourish that makes its flower beautiful and unique!
