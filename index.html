The Happiness Tree

What truly makes a nation happy — is it wealth, strong communities, good health, personal freedom, generosity, or trust in institutions?
And why do some countries bloom beautifully with very little, while others wilt despite having everything?

This visualisation presents the World Happiness Report (2005–2023) as a living tree. Each branch represents a world region, each flower a country, and each petal a factor contributing to happiness. Use the factor filters below to explore what drives happiness across nations. Hover or click on flowers for details, click branches to explore regions, and slide through the years to watch how happiness evolves over time.
